        <div class="copy">
            In the depths of Pacific Northwest winter weather, naturally my mind wanders to happy memories of our region's past outdoor excursions.  This year I was thinking about Washington's Olympic coast.

            This post is in All Ideas Welcome status at the moment, so let's have a discussion on where to go!
            <div style="border-top:1px dotted #777;margin-top:6px;padding-top:6px;margin-right:145px;" class="miseperator">
                This <a rel="nofollow" href="https://www.google.com/maps/search/campground+-national/@47.8431793,-124.5101837,10z/data=!4m4!2m3!5m1!4e9!6e1!5m1!1e4?hl=en">Google Maps link</a> shows a few highly-rated non-National Park (taking the government shutdown into consideration) campgrounds to get the ball rolling.  I'm completely unfamiliar with this corner of the big wide world, so anyone who's had camping experiences in this area is encouraged to speak out.
